**Vision**

In line with MOEâ€™s Science Curriculum Framework, our vision is for students who have undergone the Primary Science experience in New Town to display the following attributes:

a. Inspired by Science. 
<br>They enjoy learning Science as they see how everyday phenomena have scientific connections which make Science relevant and meaningful

b. Inquire like Scientists. 
<br>They are equipped with the Scientific concepts and skills necessary to allow them to engage in Practices of Science to conduct Scientific inquiry 

c. Innovate using Science. 
<br>They are able to apply Science to address real-world problems in school and at home
