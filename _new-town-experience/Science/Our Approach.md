<p>The New Town Science curriculum is represented by the three “IN”s – Inspire,
Inquire and Innovate.&nbsp;</p>
<p>a. Inspired by Science - Students enjoy learning Science as they see how
everyday phenomena have scientific connections which make Science relevant
and meaningful.&nbsp;</p>
<p>b. Inquire like Scientists - Students are equipped with the scientific
concepts and skills necessary to allow them to engage in Practices of Science
to conduct scientific inquiry.&nbsp;</p>
<p>c. Innovate using Science - Students are able to apply Science to address
real-world problems in school and at home.</p>
