<h2><strong>Mother Tongue Department Goals</strong></h2>
<p>Our department aims to nurture active learners and proficient users of
their Mother Tongue Languages (MTL), who acquire communication skills to
connect with others, and also to deepen their understanding of their culture.
We hope students will find joy in learning their MTL and appreciate it
as a living part of their identity and heritage.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p>We focus on the following:&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>1. Communication</strong>&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Develop confident communicators in Mother Tongue, equipped with 21st Century
Competencies for life and work.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>2. Culture</strong>&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Cultivate a deep appreciation for one’s cultural heritage as part of Singapore’s
multi-racial society.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Spark curiosity to explore the links between language and culture.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>3. Connect</strong>&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Strength meaningful connections to heritage and community.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Provide platforms for authentic use of MTL through real-life experiences.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
