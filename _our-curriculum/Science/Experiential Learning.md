Students from P3 to P6 will undergo key experiential learning projects each year to demonstrate their conceptual knowledge and practices of Science.

Students create a toy boat based on their knowledge of the properties of materials. They then try out their creations in a huge basin of water to see if their toy boat can work!
![](/images/Science_Experiential_1.jpg)

Students keep pet mealworms to observe their growth and make notes of the life cycle.
![](/images/Science_Experiential_2.jpg)

Students create a model of the human digestive system and present it in a creative way to their classmates.
![](/images/Science_Experiential_3.jpg)

Students create a toy that makes use of two types of forces and demonstrate how it works. They can also bring to class for a fun-filled lesson!
![](/images/Science_Experiential_4.jpg)
