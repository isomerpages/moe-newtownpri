<h4><strong>Speech and Drama</strong></h4>
<p>Speech and Drama CCA believes in building confidence and creativity through
performance. Students learn skills such as public speaking, voice projection,
expression, and stage presence while exploring drama techniques like role-play,
improvisation, and script work. At New Town Primary School’s Speech and
Drama Club, we aim to foster students’ teamwork, develop their imagination
and grow their love for the performing arts.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Objectives</strong>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Speech and Drama students will be developed in these five key areas:&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Communicate clearly and effectively, focusing on clear articulation, voice
modulation, and vocal projection.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Express emotions and ideas through various forms of presentation and drama
performance to enhance their creativity and imagination.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Work collaboratively in a group for presentation and ensemble for drama
performances, emphasizing the importance of cooperation and active listening.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Learn and apply key drama techniques such as improvisation, character
building, and script interpretation, stagecraft and scriptwriting in service
of enhancing performance, for example prop-making, sets and costumes.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Build confidence by providing opportunities for public speaking, performance
and other drama related activities (e.g. prop-making).&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Program Highlights</strong>&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Drama Workshops</strong> – learning acting techniques, voice projection,
and stage movement&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Public Speaking &amp; Storytelling Sessions</strong> – building
confidence in expressing ideas&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Readers’ Theatre &amp; Scripted Performances</strong> – practicing
teamwork through ensemble acting&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Improvisation Games</strong> – sparking creativity and quick thinking&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Scriptwriting Activities</strong> – encouraging students to create
their own short plays&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Showcase Performances</strong> – presenting skits or plays during
school events or assemblies&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Participation in Competitions/Festivals</strong> – representing
the school in drama or oratory contests&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Peer Feedback &amp; Reflection</strong> – learning to give and
receive constructive feedback&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
