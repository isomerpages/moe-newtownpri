<h3>Red Cross (Youth)</h3>
<p>Red Cross Youth (Links) is a uniformed group CCA that nurtures young leaders
with strong character, life-saving skills, and a heart for service. Guided
by the Red Cross ethos and humanitarian values, our Links learn to show
compassion, empathy, and responsibility while gaining practical knowledge
in first aid and community service.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Objectives</strong>&nbsp;
<br>Red Cross Youth (Links) aims to:&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Equip members with essential first aid skills such as treating simple
wounds and tying arm slings.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Develop values of compassion, empathy, and civic responsibility through
service to others.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Build confidence, discipline, and leadership qualities in a supportive
team environment.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Foster adaptability, resilience, and teamwork through structured training
and service-learning experiences.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Programme Highlights</strong>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Our Links are given opportunities to apply their knowledge and skill in
competitions, such as<strong> </strong>First Aid Championship, Disaster
Risk Reduction Championship, and Junior Civil Defence Lionhearter Challenge.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p>Besides weekly trainings, our Links also actively contribute to our school
and local community through various Values-In-Action activities, like serving
as first-aiders during our school’s Open House and Sports Day, participating
in the Buona Vista Community Centre’s National Day Observance Ceremony,
and celebrating Chinese New Year with the seniors from St. Andrews Nursing
Home.</p>
