<h4><strong>Arrival and Dismissal</strong></h4>
<p>Parents should <u>not</u> alight their children outside the school gate
near the pedestrian crossing. This is to avoid traffic congestion along
Tanglin Halt Road.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>

for the first <strong>ten minutes </strong>of the school dismissal.&nbsp;</p>

</ul>
<ul>
<li>
