<h4><strong>Sign up to be a PSG Member and join us!</strong></h4>
<p>Hello, amazing parents! Want to be more involved in your child’s primary
school journey? Join our Parent Support Group (PSG) and make a difference!
Your support is always valued, regardless of the amount of time you are
able to contribute.</p>
<p><strong>Signing up is easy!</strong> Just follow these simple steps:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Fill out the registration form in this link – <a href="https://go.gov.sg/psgnewtown" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/psgnewtown</a>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Submit your details – It only takes a minute!</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Stay connected – We’ll reach out with updates on activities and events.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>No experience? No problem! Whether you love planning events, sharing ideas,
or simply lending a helping hand, there’s a place for you in our PSG family.</p>
<p>Let’s work together to create a fun, supportive, and enriching environment
for our children!</p>
<p>Join us today – We look forward to welcoming you to the PSG family!</p>
